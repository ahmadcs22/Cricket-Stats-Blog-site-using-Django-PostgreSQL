Tournament History

Hosting the 2026 Spectacle

The 10th edition of the ICC Men's T20 World Cup was an absolute thriller, co-hosted by India and Sri Lanka from February 7 to March 8, 2026.

Spanning across 8 iconic venues—from the massive Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad (which hosted the electric Final) to the historic R. Premadasa Stadium in Colombo—the tournament brought together 20 nations competing in 55 matches.

India made history on home soil, securing their third T20 World Cup title and becoming the first team to win back-to-back championships. Meanwhile, debutants like Italy experienced the global stage for the very first time.
