About The Platform

Your dedicated hub for analyzing the numbers, tracking the rankings, and reliving the highlights of the historic 2026 ICC Men's T20 World Cup.

The 2026 Tournament

Co-hosted by the cricketing powerhouses of India and Sri Lanka, the 10th edition of the T20 World Cup brought together 20 nations for a month of thrilling, high-stakes cricket from February to March.

From dramatic group-stage upsets to India securing their third championship title in Ahmedabad, the tournament was a spectacular showcase of global talent. This platform was built to preserve those moments through comprehensive data and interactive statistics.

  • 🏟️ Matches: 55 Games
  • 🏆 Champions: India
